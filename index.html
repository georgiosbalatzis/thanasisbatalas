About Me

Passionate about creating music that resonates with the soul.

I'm Thanasis Batalas, a vocal artist, performer, and songwriter dedicated to crafting authentic musical experiences. With a unique blend of contemporary and traditional Greek influences, my sound celebrates cultural heritage while maintaining a signature style that audiences connect with.

My journey in music began over a decade ago, and I've been fortunate to collaborate with talented artists and producers who have helped shape my artistic vision.
